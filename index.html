Investment Approach

Warren Buffet and Artificial Intelligence

Our investing philosophy is not far from Warren Buffet’s idea of value investing. Investing in wonderful business at or below market value. While Buffet’s strategy consistently generates good returns over a long period of time with the reduced risk it's size make reduces it’s agility. VillageTree have the benefit of agility and our ability to leverage artificial intelligence to maximize gains allows us to mitigate risks and generate returns from 15% to 48% annually.

How it got started

I remember my father lost half of his life savings in a mutual fund offered by his company. Devastated, I questioned the broker for not doing more to protect his money. I later discovered that some people got advice to exit the market before the downturn and my father didn’t. These people got special treatment because they had millions of dollars in the bank. The same service just isn’t accessible for my father.

In 2005, I worked as a computer scientist, developing unmanned aerial control stations for the US and Canadian military. I also specialize in the area of voice recognition and develop controls for robotics for the purpose of landmine detection and neutralization in third world countries for the Canadian government. I was captivated by the power of artificial intelligence and how we can use it to save lives. Around the same, I learn about the stock market. Both paths of research seem to be very different from the other. After my father’s misfortune, I realize if what my village need doesn’t exist, it’s up to me to create it. That is how VillageTree got started.

The Village Vacation Fund

No one should have to choose between wealth and happiness. Amy was getting frustrated with friends and families who don’t have time to sit down for a coffee. She remembers scheduling a coffee 3 weeks out with her best friend only to have it canceled the day before. Frustrated, Amy created the Village Vacation Fund. Friends and family were invited to participate in the fund by contributing their vacation budget, for example, $2000. Once 100% of the return is achieved, the principle is never touched. All gains made goes towards an annual vacation. VillageTree does not take any fee or commission for this service. The catch is everyone must go on vacation together. The intent of the fund to have annual vacation paid for and quality time enjoyed by the village.

A word from our founder, Amy Giang

“I am overwhelmed with gratitude, and my heart is full. My family and I immigrated to Canada in the late 80s with less than $1 and none of us spoke a word of English. We commit ourselves to never take a penny from the system and never did. My parents worked hard and sacrifice everything so my brothers and I could have an education. We’ve been offered nothing but kindness by all those we met. The few who were not nice to us offered us the most kindness. For because of them, we learned what it meant to be disappointed, how to persevere, how to figure out what we still needed to learn, and how to patiently wait for the next opportunity. Special thanks to my village which includes my family, friends, my team, customers, supporters and community for your unwavering support!”
